Bible Love Devotion Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Bible Love Devotion ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect and Process

We do not collect, store, or retain any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration — You do not need to create an account to use our app.
  • No personal data collection — We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.
  • No usage analytics — We do not use any analytics SDKs (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.).
  • No device identifiers — We do not collect device IDs, advertising identifiers, or location data.

Exception — AI Devotional Feature: When you use the AI devotional feature, your text input is temporarily transmitted to our server and processed by a third-party AI provider to generate your devotional. This data is not stored after the response is generated. See Section 4 for details.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never sent to our servers:

  • Your language preference (English/Chinese)
  • Your dark mode preference
  • Your bookmarked Bible verses

You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

Photos Library access: When you choose to save a verse or devotional card as an image, the app writes that generated image to your device's Photos library so you can share it to other apps (such as WeChat) yourself. This uses an "Add Photos Only" permission — the app can only write images you explicitly save, and never reads, scans, uploads, or accesses any other photos in your library.

4. AI-Generated Devotional Content

When you use the AI devotional feature, the following occurs:

  • Your text input (describing your situation) is sent to our server to generate a personalized devotional.
  • We process this input solely for generating the devotional response.
  • We do not log, store, or retain your input text after the response is generated.
  • AI-generated content is for encouragement only and does not constitute professional counseling or religious authority.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • AI Provider: We use AI services (such as DeepSeek or Anthropic Claude) to generate devotional content. These providers may temporarily process your input according to their own privacy policies.
  • Purchases & Subscriptions (Apple App Store / RevenueCat): When you purchase or restore Premium, your transaction is processed by Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat to verify your subscription and apply your entitlement. RevenueCat receives a randomly generated, anonymous app identifier and purchase metadata — no name, email, or contact information. This is not linked to your identity. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate any advertising networks, social media SDKs, or tracking tools.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any data transmitted between your device and our servers, including HTTPS encryption.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Your Regional Privacy Rights

Because we do not collect, store, or process any personal information (see Section 2), most regional data-protection rights do not apply — there is no personal data about you in our possession. This includes the right to access, correct, delete, or opt out of the sale or sharing of personal data under laws such as the CCPA/CPRA (California, United States), the UK-GDPR and EU-GDPR (United Kingdom / European Union), PIPEDA (Canada), the Privacy Act (Australia), and the PDPA (Singapore / Malaysia).
